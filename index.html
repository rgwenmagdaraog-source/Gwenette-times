WHAT IS HER STORY?

Childhood Photo

First day of kindergarten

G wen always tried her best in school and put effort in everything she did. At only five years old, she moved from the Philippines to the UAE, where she had to adapted to a new environment and meet new people.

From kindergarten to becoming an senior high school student in FEPS Halwan, she continues to develop her skills and grow as a person. Through multiple experiences and the different activities she joined at school, Gwen strives to become an individual who is prepared for opportunities in the future, while making her parents proud.

Future Goals and Aspirations

As a student, Gwen Magdaraog hopes to pursue a degree in Dentistry (DMD) and build a career focused on helping others while supporting her family. Inspired by her cousin, a dentist who owns two clinics in the Philippines, she continues to develop her skills and gain new experiencesto help her achieve her dream course in a big university.
Beyond her career goals, she dreams of traveling the world and exploring different cultures. Having already visited the Philippines, the UAE, Oman, and Georgia, she appreciates experiencing different lifestyles and perspectives.

The future is built by those who keep working for it. — Rebeca Gwen Magdaraog
